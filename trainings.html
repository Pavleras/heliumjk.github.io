Team Kanban Practitioner

TKP
Learn the basics of the Kanban Method. Design and implement a Team Kanban Task Board. Learn visualization of different types of work and associated risk.

Kanban System Design

KSD
Learn the Kanban Method and the concepts of flow, pull and collaborative improvement.

Kanban Management Professional

KMPII
This class focuses on the rollout and daily operation of a Kanban System. It shows how to manage and evolve a kanban initiative over time without trauma. The class also covers recommended meetings and setting policies.
